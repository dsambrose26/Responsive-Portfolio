About Me

I met a friend who works as a Data Scientist and is passionate about programming, who introduced me to the world of Full Stack Development. I am now a Developer In Training, focused on finding my niche. What is most fulfilling about this challenge is the opportunity to learn an array of technical skills that are constantly adjusting within the demands of so many growing technical fields. I am a committed hardworker, and would value the opportunity to partner with efforts in the community that improve the human experience. I have a strong background in management and all things program related.

My other interests range from real estate to coaching football, but my family is the center of my life. One day I hope to use my organizational and community building skills to advance the technical systems within a company.
